About us

Our Story

We started off as a student group for CS 4261 at Georgia Tech, where we noticed that many online platforms do not have the most authentic reviews. Many sites such as Google Reviews, Yelp, and TripAdvisor are inundated with reviews about the same tourist traps, making it a chore to plan a trip that gets the full authentic experience out of a city. As tourists ourselves, we noticed how hard it was to get full, end-to-end experiences that were easy to use and easy to share.

            

Stumble solves these problems by creating a dedicated social platform for locals to be able to share their expertise, and allows tourists to communicate amongst themselves and with locals using our groupchats.
